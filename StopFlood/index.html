We've Got What You Need!

Are you protected from water damage? In many areas flood damage has surpassed fire as the leading cost of repairs. Most homes and offices have fire extinguishers but very few homes or offices are equipped with automatic water shut-off systems. In the past, water shut-off systems were quite expensive and not easily transferred to another home or office. We at StopFlood Manufacturers Inc. have developed a very economical, easy to install system that detects leaks and shuts off the main water supply within 3 to 5 seconds. The system comes with ten small wireless sensors which typically would be installed under the hot water tank, washing machine, dishwasher, sinks, behind toilets etc. Each sensor has a battery that has approximately a five year life-span and are inexpensive and easy to replace. When the leak is addressed, a simple touch to the reset button turns the water back on. Get your own StopFlood system today and have a safer flood-free home!
